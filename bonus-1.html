2

Stage 3 — Email Capture & Nurture

Not every lead is ready to book a call. Use a lead magnet linked in your DM sequence or bio to capture emails. Then Kit.com (or your email platform) nurtures them with automated sequences until they're ready to buy.

Best-performing lead magnets for Instagram audiences:

  • A short checklist or "starter kit" PDF relevant to your niche
  • A quick video training (10–15 minutes) on a specific pain point
  • A free tool, template, or calculator
  • Access to a private community or waitlist

Stage 4 — Close (CallFluent AI)

When a lead books a call (via Calendly, Tidycal, or any booking link), CallFluent AI handles the qualification and close automatically. Your AI phone agent already knows your offer, handles objections, and books or closes — 24/7.
